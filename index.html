Why It Matters

Not every roof is solar-ready. Some homeowners don't want the long-term commitment, and for others, the financials just never lined up. Summit’s Community Solar makes it possible for everyone to benefit from solar energy—without putting a panel on your roof.

How It Works

Homes that Summit has already installed often generate more energy than they need. That excess power can now be sent to your home through the existing power grid. We take care of managing the transfer, billing you only for the solar energy you use—at a contracted rate that saves you money every month.
